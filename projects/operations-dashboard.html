Workforce Optimization Systems

A practical analytics chamber built from real operational pressure: payroll accuracy, scheduling risk, coverage gaps, compliance, and workforce forecasting.

Problem

Operational systems break down when scheduling, payroll, compliance, and staffing risks are tracked in separate places. The goal was to make those risks visible early enough to correct them before they became client-facing problems.

Method

Built Excel and Power BI dashboards that integrated payroll, scheduling, and labor data. The system tracked overtime, coverage gaps, payroll issues, staffing risks, and compliance metrics so supervisors could see where action was needed.

Impact

The dashboard and process improvements supported an 80% reduction in payroll discrepancies and improved workforce planning. KPI tracking and forecasting also helped reduce understaffed shifts by 60% through better visibility into coverage risk.

What This Shows

This project demonstrates the bridge between leadership and analytics: understanding messy operational behavior, designing usable reporting systems, and translating data into better execution.
