Operations Dashboard Systems

Operational analytics work connecting staffing, scheduling, payroll accuracy, compliance reporting, KPI tracking, and workforce forecasting.

Problem

Operational systems create constant uncertainty: staffing demand, payroll errors, open posts, compliance issues, and coverage gaps. This work turns that mess into dashboards, KPIs, and forecasting workflows.

Data Problems

  • Payroll and scheduling discrepancies.
  • Coverage gaps and understaffed shifts.
  • Compliance metrics and reporting quality.
  • Resource allocation under real operational constraints.

Methods

  • Excel and Power BI dashboards.
  • Forecasting models for staffing demand.
  • KPI tracking for overtime, coverage, payroll, and compliance.
  • Automated reporting workflows to reduce manual work.

Impact

In the resume version of this work, the dashboards and tracking systems are connected to major improvements in payroll accuracy and staffing visibility, including an 80% reduction in payroll discrepancies.

Stack

Excel, Power BI, forecasting, KPI dashboards, payroll reconciliation, scheduling analytics, compliance reporting.
