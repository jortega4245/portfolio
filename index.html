The project engineers modeling features, explores cyclical behavior through periodograms, applies autoregressive models, and develops structured financial interpretations with a fine-tuned 3B LLM.

A mean-reversion pairs trading model using cointegration, ADF tests, z-score thresholds, and backtesting in Python. The strategy incorporates uncertainty around half-life estimates and optimized entry/exit rules for stronger risk-adjusted performance.
